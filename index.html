Sanket Shah

Research Engineer

Singapore Management University

I am a Research Engineer at Singapore Management University advised by Prof. Pradeep Varakantham. My current work focuses on using Reinforcement Learning to solve real-world problems in Transportation and Security. I am more broadly interested in understanding how to use my knowledge of AI to be of use to society.

Prior to this, I spent a year at Microsoft Research India during which I worked on Natural Language Processing with Dr. Sundararajan Sellamanickam and Information and Communications Technology for Development with Dr. Colin Scott and Dr. Bill Thies.
